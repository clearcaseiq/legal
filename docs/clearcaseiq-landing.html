Why ClearCaseIQ

My father passed away from complications following a quadruple bypass surgery. In that moment of grief and shock, our family didn't know where to turn or what to do. That experience showed me how overwhelming it is for families to navigate the legal system during the most difficult time of their lives.

I started ClearCaseIQ so that no family has to feel lost. We're building a platform that makes it simple for people to share their story and documents and for attorneys to respond quickly with clarity and care.

About ClearCaseIQ

ClearCaseIQ is a modern legal technology platform designed to streamline case intake, assessment, and attorney workflows. Built for personal injury firms, it combines AI powered analysis with intuitive tools to help attorneys evaluate cases faster and manage their practice more effectively.

From intake to settlement, ClearCaseIQ provides a single platform for managing leads, assessing case viability, organizing evidence, and collaborating with your team. We're building the tools that today's PI firms need to work smarter and grow their practice.

How It Works

Key Features

Smart Case Intake

Streamlined plaintiff intake with guided questionnaires, document uploads, and evidence collection. Case type specific forms ensure you capture the right information from day one.

AI Powered Analysis

Automated viability scoring, liability assessment, and damages prediction. Prioritize high value matters and reduce time spent on low viability cases.

Attorney Dashboard

Centralized view of leads, cases, and workflows. Tier based routing, quality metrics, and analytics help you manage your pipeline efficiently.

Evidence Management

Secure document storage, medical chronology, case preparation tools, and settlement benchmarks. Keep everything organized and accessible.

Referrals & Co Counsel

Manage case shares, referrals, and co counsel workflows. Accept, decline, and track collaboration in one place.

Demand Letters & Documents

AI assisted demand letter drafting, invoice generation, and document export. Streamline paperwork and focus on your clients.
